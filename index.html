Birria Pizza

Birria pizza is a trendy fusion dish that combines traditional Mexican flavors with a classic Italian favorite. The dish features a pizza crust topped with a layer of birria, a spicy and flavorful Mexican stew traditionally made with beef, chilies, and other aromatic spices.
